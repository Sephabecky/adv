Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Makueni County and also any other county nationwide or outside country. We also provide services to neighboring counties by appointment.

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We respond to phone calls and WhatsApp messages immediately during business hours. Email inquiries are responded to within 24 hours.

Do you offer emergency farm consultation?

Yes, we offer emergency consultation services for urgent farm issues like pest outbreaks, disease outbreaks, or irrigation system failures. Call our emergency line for immediate assistance.

Can I visit your office without an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure that the appropriate agronomist is available to assist you with your specific needs.
