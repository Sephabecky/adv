About Aaron Agronomy Services

Transforming agriculture in Makueni County through innovation, expertise, and dedication

Our Story

Welcome to Aaron Agronomy Services, where innovation meets excellence in agricultural solutions. With a rich history and a commitment to sustainable farming practices, we have emerged as a trusted partner for farmers seeking top-notch agronomic services.

Today, we are proud to be a trusted partner for farmers seeking to improve their yields, adopt sustainable practices, and build profitable agricultural enterprises.

OUR HISTORY

Founded in the year 2015, Aaron Agronomy Services has deep roots in the agricultural sector in Makueni county. Aaron Ndisya, the visionary behind the company, started with a passion for transforming traditional farming methods into efficient, modern practices.

Over the years, our company has grown steadily, evolving with advancements in agronomic science and technology. Today, we stand as a beacon of progress, managing a pixie farm with over 1300 trees and offering a comprehensive suite of services to farmers across diverse landscapes.

Our Mission

At Aaron Agronomy Services, our mission is to empower farmers with cutting-edge agronomic solutions that enhance productivity, sustainability, and profitability. We strive to be at the forefront of agricultural innovation, providing our clients with the knowledge and tools they need to thrive in a rapidly changing world.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a global leader in agronomy services, contributing significantly to the development of a sustainable and resilient agricultural ecosystem. We envision a future where farmers leverage the latest technologies and practices to achieve bountiful harvests while preserving the environment for generations to come.
